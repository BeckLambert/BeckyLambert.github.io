About the developer

My name is Becky Lambert, I had the pleasure of living in Summit County (which some like to refer to as the real life Neverland) for seven years and I wanted to create something that showed off how much I love it. After trying several different layouts and ideas that I ended up not liking. I decided to go with the saying "pictures are worth a thousand words".

This website is here simply to show off the beauties that I've been able to see and enjoy throughout my time in this county. As you can imagine I've taken hundreds and hundreds of pictures. This gallery is just a few of my favorites. I hope you enjoy it as much as I do!
